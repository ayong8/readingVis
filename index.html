readingVIS

Visualizing the aggregated reading patterns

Activity timeline

The activity timeline identifies the general pattern of students’ reading sequences over time. The x-axis is a series of pages from required textbooks, and the y-axis is the given semester period. Each path represents a student’s reading sequence over all required materials during a semester. Blue circles are quizzes they solved, and the red line is the midterm day as an important event.

Activities by time

This layout helps users to understand the temporal pattern of students’ behavior along with cyclic events. Each bar represents how many reading activities occurred. You can select the daily or hourly view on the radio button.

Student list

This list controls the interaction. Select a student to highlight the information

Clustering

Clustering is based on the similarity of students' reading and quizz answering activities. Each color represents the cluster each student belong. The final plot is transformed into two dimensional space by Multi-dimensional scaling(MDS).
